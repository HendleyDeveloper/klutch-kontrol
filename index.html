What I Offer

I offer the platform for you to learn a new skill for life in a safe and pleasant environment. Learning takes place at your own pace. Lessons are centered around you, the customer.

Manual Driving Lessons

All lessons are delivered in a modern manual car on a 1-to-1 basis. Lessons are delivered to your own needs and skill set. Passing your driving test in a manual car gives you the option of driving both manual and automatic cars when you have passed.

Pass Plus

I offer Pass Plus training which takes at least 6 hours. It has 6 modules, covering the following driving situations:

  • in town
  • in all weathers
  • on rural roads
  • at night
  • on dual carriageways
  • on motorways

You will not take a test but you'll be assessed throughout the course. To pass you'll have to reach the required standard in all modules. This course could lower your insurance costs.

Motorway Lessons

Motorway lessons can give you the knowledge and confidence to be a competent and safe motorway driver.

One-on-One Tuition

All lessons are carried out on a one-to-one basis. With lessons delivered to your needs and driving experience.
